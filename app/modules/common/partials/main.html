Data Processing

You may navigate to Create and then go to Upload Datasets in order to import the data tables into the Policy Compass system and define the indicator that this data represents.

Data Visualisation

Applying datasets to metrics describes a real world situation. The selection of metrics and their construction or the specific selection of data already contains implications on the reasons or the processes behind it. However, you need ways to finding events that could have (had) a causal effect on the real world situation represented by the data.

In Policy Compass you can visualise datasets using different graphical forms, such as line graphs, pie, bar or map charts..
